About Us


Who are we?

We are a multi-disciplinary club that seeks to unite students from varied backgrounds in the pursuit of design learning and excellence. Hosting both large and small events on and off campus, we work to connect current students with alumni, design professionals, and resources and prepare them to step out confidently into the working world.

Mission

Design Careers Club at UC Davis aims to empower students to take bold risks not only in their designs, but in their career aspirations. Each year, we provide three main events: Internship Night during Fall Quarter, Alumni Networking Day during Winter Quarter, and our big event, the Design Career Fair during Spring Quarter.

Preparing for the Design Career Fair


    Research, Research, Research

      Look at the companies that will be attending which interest you most. This is a great way to figure out which companies you’re compatible with and the key qualities they’re looking for.

    Refine your Resume

      Whether you’ve fine-tuned for the 100th time or just started creating your resume. Do your best to finish before the Design Career Fair. Check in with advisors, bounce ideas with friends or ask for feedback from professors. Remember, the Resume Review Booth will be available at the fair if you have any uncertanty!

    Assemble your Portfolio

      If you have a collection of work you’d like to show off to employers this is the perfect time to highlight some of those creative pieces.

    Ask Away!

      Our speakers and panelists come from a diverse set of backgrounds, which means you have a fantastic opportunity to ask the questions only they could answer.

      If you come up with questions during the event feel free to ask on Twitter at #UCDavisDCF2018!

How to get here


The Design Career Fair will be held at the Art Annex

Art Annex
Davis, CA 95616

By Bike & Bus:

Some bike parking will be available directly in front of the Art Annex, but given how many attendees we expect, that will fill up! Please park by Shields Library and walk across the street to the Art Annex if that occurs.

The Silo Terminal is 2-3 minute walk to the Art Annex. When walking from the Memorial Union Terminal, it is about 7-8 minutes.

By Car:

Parking is free on the weekends, but the Art Annex parking lot will be reserved for companies setting up booths. For attendees, please park in Lot 5 and stroll through the Arboretum. Once you arrive at the parking lot, follow the signs to the Art Annex.
